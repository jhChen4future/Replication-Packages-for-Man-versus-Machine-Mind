This is the last part of the experiment.

In this part, you will be assign as either Proposer or Responder and then make decisions described as below.

There will be $10 for a proposer to decide to allocate between yourself and your partner. Once the proposer makes the offer the responder can either accept it or reject it. If the responder chooses to accept it, the allocation happens and both of you will get the proposed money. However, if the responder decides to reject the offer, the proposal fails and both of you will receive $0. In the next part, You will make decisions as both proposer and responder before you know your roles, and the system will randomly decides your role, and exercise your decision.
